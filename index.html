How to make Turkish pomegranate tea

Pomegranate is called the king of fruits in the East, it is rich in vitamins, it is known as a symbol of fertility and abundance. Pomegranate tea has a delicate taste and health benefits.It is important to be able to brew the drink correctly, keeping its best properties.

How to extract pomegranate juice
You will need pomegranate juice to make tea. You can get it in several ways:
1. Through a juicer. The device will squeeze it out not only from the grains, but also from the partitions, and the juice will taste a little bitter.
2. To prevent the juice from having an unpleasant aftertaste, knead the fruit in your hands, make a small hole in the fruit and pour out the liquid.
3. You can buy ready-made pomegranate juice, its quality will differ from that prepared at home.

How to choose a pomegranate for tea
When choosing fruits, pay attention to the appearance.
- Ripe pomegranates should be dry, a smooth crust is a sign of an unripe fruit;
- The place where the flower grew, the "bottom" of the pomegranate, should not be green;
- Choose hard fruits.

How to brew pomegranate tea
Making pomegranate tea is simple:
1. You will need 2 parts pomegranate juice and the same amount of black or green tea, 1 part sugar syrup.
2. Combine all ingredients with a blender.
3. You can add a few mint leaves, lime juice, or a pinch of cinnamon to the finished drink.
You can make pomegranate tea from leaves and flowers.To do this, take a spoonful of flowers and fruit leaves, pour boiling water over and leave for 15 minutes, strain.

Ready-made pomegranate tea from Turkey is delicious and can be bought at the store. The raw material for the drink is tea plantations on the eastern coast of the Black Sea.
To get the rich taste of this tea, you need to brew it correctly:
1.Use tea utensils to prepare the drink.
2. Use cold water, it contains more oxygen and the tea will be tastier;
3. Add 2 teaspoons per glass to the kettle.
4.Pour water when it just starts to boil, without waiting for boiling over.
5. It is recommended to infuse the drink from 5 minutes.

Pomegranate tea from Turkey is brewed in a special double teapot, water boils in the lower part, slightly pouring over the upper one. There are several spoons of tea in the upper part.
You can make such tea at home by putting the dishes on the fire, wait for the boil and add the tea, cook for 1 minute.
